Recommendations to Lower Electricity Usage

1. Turn Off Lights

When you leave a room, make sure to turn off the lights to save energy.

2. Optimize Thermostat Settings

Set your thermostat a few degrees lower in winter and higher in summer.

3. Unplug Devices

Unplug chargers and devices when not in use to avoid phantom energy usage.

4. Use Energy-Efficient Appliances

Consider switching to appliances that are energy-efficient.

5. Take Shorter Showers

Reduce water heating costs by taking shorter showers.

6. Regular Maintenance

Keep HVAC systems well-maintained for optimal performance.
